Brief Study Overview
A Phase II/III Adaptive Seamless Design, Randomized, Controlled Trial To Evaluate Safety And Immunogenicity of 2 Dose-Regimen Of BBV87 Chikungunya Vaccine In Healthy Subjects Aged 12 to 65 years In Panama, Colombia, and Thailand.Primary Objectives :• To evaluate the safety and tolerability profile of BBV87 40μg at 0-6 M schedule (Applicable to part A) and to evaluate the safety and tolerability profile across two (2) dose strengths of BBV87 (i.e., 20μg and 40μg) at 0-28 days schedule. (Applicable to parts B and C).• To evaluate the immunogenicity 28 days PD2 (i.e., GMTs) at 1 month interval across two (2) dose strengths (i.e., 20μg vs 40μg).(Applicable to parts B and C)Secondary Objectives including long term follow up 6 months post last dose. (Applicable to parts B and C)parts A, B, and C)Relevant attributes:Population: first intake in pediatric population (12-17 years old ) .Formulation: The component tested is the BBV87 20 and 40 micrograms. BBV87 is an inactivated Chikungunya virus manufactured by Bharat Biotech International Limited.Schedule: The patients will be vaccinated at 2 doses (each 6 months)
